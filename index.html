How it works
A number in four steps.
No carrier store, no paperwork, no waiting. From signup to your first message in under two minutes.
01
Pick a country & number
Browse available local numbers across our launch countries. Real local prefixes — not virtual numbers with hidden limitations. Choose the one that fits where you are or where you're going.
02
eSIM delivered instantly
No physical SIM card, no shipping address, no waiting. Your number arrives as an eSIM profile. Scan a QR code in your phone's settings and you're live within seconds.
03
Register on any app
Use your new number to register or verify on any internet-based messaging or calling app — the same way you'd use any mobile number.
WhatsApp Viber Signal Messenger Telegram
04
Manage from one dashboard
Add numbers for your whole team, assign roles, track usage, and manage billing — all from the tel-os web or mobile app. No IT team required. Cancel any number, any time.
